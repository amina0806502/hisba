Welcome to the Hisbah Crime Management and reporting System

This system allows hisbah enforcement agencies and citizens to manage crime reports efficiently, track cases, and ensure justice is served.

This service is made available for hisbah. It is design to make the people of hisbah aware from crime.Here people can make complaint online. In order to do so, first they have to register themselves on the system. User can also view profile and check status using this system.
